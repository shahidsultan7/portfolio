About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Shahid Sultan Hajam, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Technology at the Central University of Kashmir. My academic journey has equipped me with a Doctor of Philosophy in Engineering, an M.Tech in Information Technology, and a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering.

My research interests primarily lie in the areas of Internet of Things (IoT), Fog Computing, Resource Optimization, Machine Learning, and Smart Cities. I am passionate about exploring and contributing to cutting-edge technologies that address real-world challenges.

I am committed to both teaching and research, striving to foster a deep understanding of information technology among my students and to advance knowledge in my fields of expertise.
